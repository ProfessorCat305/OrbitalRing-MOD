## 模组说明 | Mod Introduction

[![petlzIP.png](https://s41.ax1x.com/2026/04/04/petlzIP.png)](https://imgchr.com/i/petlzIP)
修改包括但不限于：
- 可最终产出煤和原油以及其他物品的生物线配方
- 更加鼓励建设外星分基地的黄糖阶段
- 新增铀矿，硫磺矿，辉银矿，深层熔岩，岩浆海，原油海，甲烷海等新资源
- 核裂变科技线
- 从太空电梯到物流港以及其他同步轨道设施的航天科技线
- 将同步轨道设施连接起来并提供更强功能的行星级巨构星环科技线
- 数个行星不同的星球特质或隐藏
- 在原版通关后新增更高要求的真·通关系列科技
- 制造台增产插件和依靠星环单台全球生效的全球增产插件
- 等难以一一列数的修改

The modifications include, but are not limited to:
- Biological thread formulas that can ultimately produce coal, crude oil, and other items
- The brown sugar stage, which further encourages the construction of extraterrestrial sub-bases
- New resources such as uranium ore, sulfur ore, argentite, deep lava, magma sea, crude oil sea, and methane sea have been added
- Nuclear fission technology line
- Aerospace technology line encompassing space elevators, logistics ports, and other synchronous orbit facilities
- The planetary-scale megastructure orbitalring technology line that connects synchronous orbit facilities and provides enhanced functionality
- Several characteristics or hidden aspects of exoplanets
- After completing the original version, a new series of technologies with higher requirements, known as the True Connection series, has been added
- Manufacture production increase plugins for the manufacturing platform and rely on the global production increase plugins that take effect on a single platform globally through the star ring
- Other modifications that are difficult to list one by one
